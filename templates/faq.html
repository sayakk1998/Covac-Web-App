You can log into the portal using the link www.covac.in and click on the “Register/Sign In yourself” tab to register yourself for COVID-19 vaccination.

There is no authorised mobile app for registering for vaccination in India except Aarogya Setu. You need to log into the portal. Alternatively, you can also register for vaccination through the Aarogya Setu App.

All citizens aged 18 years and above can register for vaccination.

Vaccination Centres provide for a limited number of on-spot registration slots every day. Citizens aged 45 years and above can schedule appointments online or walk-in to vaccination centres. However, Citizens aged 18-44 years should mandatorily register themselves and schedule appointment online before going to vaccination centre. In general, all citizens are recommended to register online and schedule vaccination in advance for a hassle-free vaccination experience.

You can book appointment for vaccination through portal after logging-in to the Portal through your login credentials. The system will show vaccination centres that allow vaccination as per the age of the citizen entered in the registration portal.

You can search in portal for the vaccination centre nearest to your location by either searching through PIN code or by choosing the State and the District.

A COVID Vaccine Certificate (CVC) issued by the government offers an assurance to the beneficiary on the vaccination, type of vaccine used, and the provisional certificate also provides the next vaccination due. It also is an evidence for the citizen to prove to any entities which may require proof of vaccination specially in case of travel. Vaccination not only protects individuals from disease, but also reduces their risk of spreading the virus. Therefore, there could be a requirement in future to produce certificate for certain kind of social interactions and international travel.

You should carry your identity proof specified by you at the time of registration on the portal and a printout/screenshot of your appointment slip.
